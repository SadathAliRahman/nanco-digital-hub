Who We Are

Nanco Paints is Kerala's premier ISO certified paint manufacturing company. Established with a passion for quality and durability, we specialize in high-performance coatings, emulsions, and waterproofing solutions. Our products are formulated to withstand the harsh coastal monsoons and intense sun, ensuring your homes remain vibrant and protected year after year.

We believe in empowering local paint workers and providing unparalleled support to our factory outlets across the state.
